About EVENT EASE

EVENT EASE is a simple, lightweight portal to streamline college event management. It helps student bodies and coordinators plan schedules, promote happenings across campus, and guide attendees to the right venues.

Our Goals

  • Simplify event discovery for students and visitors
  • Provide clear, accessible schedules and locations
  • Encourage participation across cultural, sports, and technical domains

How It Works

Browse events by category, check highlights on the home page, and use the built‑in map to reach venues faster. For queries or collaborations, reach us via the contact page.
